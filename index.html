Crystal Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the
game.
There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal
you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.
You win the game by matching your total score to the random
number. You lose the game if your total score goes above
the ran it.
The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you
click on it.
Each time the game restarts, the game will change the value of
each crystal.
